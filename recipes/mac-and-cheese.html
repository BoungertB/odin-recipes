Macaroni and Cheese

Delicious Cheesy Recipe

Who doesn't love a simple macaroni and cheese. Here's a simple recipe that makes your macaroni cheesy and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. macaroni
  • 4 tsbp butter
  • 3 tsbp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 9 oz. block sharp cheddar, shredded

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni for 8 minutes. Drain the macaroni in a collander once finished
  2. Put butter and flour into a medium pot. Whisk and heat the medium pot until the butter is melted and mixture starts bubbling. After the bubbling, continue to whisk for a minute.
  3. Pour milk into the butter and flour roux. Continue to whisk until consistency begins to feel gravy-like.
  4. Turn heat off and add the salt and hot sauce.
  5. Begin to add the shredded cheddar into the sauce and whisk.
  6. Add the macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
