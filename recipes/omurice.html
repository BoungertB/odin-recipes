Omurice(Japanese Omelette Rice)

Omurice is a popular dish that orginated from Japan. The base of the dish consists of fried rice, that is covered by a runny omelette.

Ingredients

  • Cooked white rice (preferrably day-over rice)
  • 3 eggs
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Add oil to a heated pan. Saut√® onions within the pan.
  2. Add butter to the pan, alongside the cooked white rice.
  3. Season the cooked white rice with salt, pepper, and soy sauce.
  4. Pour the rice into the rice molder.
  5. Mix eggs in a bowl then pour onto hot pan.
  6. Stir the eggs quickly and start forming into an omelette shape.
  7. Lastly place the omelette over the rice and slice through the middle.
